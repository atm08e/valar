Now recruiting skilled leaders. Do you have what it takes to be a leader? Can you coordinate nine other peons into a fighting force? Can you follow orders while giving out simpler ones? If this describes you Valar Morghulis would like to offer you  a squad leader position. What is a squad leader exactly? Well squad leaders are the people in charge of making groups within Planetside. We need you to invite members online into squads and get them headed to the right battle. Besides the already included in game benefits of being a squad leader there are also additional benefits within the outfit. If you are a successful squad leader you can become a commander. As a commander you are essentially on par with the leaders of the outfit. You are a voice in the organization of platoons and squads. You partake in the decision of the battle plans. Also as a commander it is your job to find new recruits for the outfit.
